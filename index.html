Welcome to Voidsent SMP!

Join us on Voidsent, a survival Minecraft server where you can explore, build, and survive together. Hop in at play.voidsent.net to start your adventure!

Server Features

Our server offers a balanced survival experience with land claims, custom enchants, and items, allowing you to protect your builds and explore vast collections of items with a simple yet effective plugin. We have a custom economy system, player shops, and a variety of biomes to explore. Plus, our active staff ensures smooth gameplay with regular updates and events to keep things engaging!

Rules

1. No hacking of any kind.

2. No doxing or leaking other players' information.

3. No excessive use of derogatory language.

4. No griefing or stealing. (except in factions)

5. No duplication machines.

6. No spamming in chat or using inappropriate language.

7. No border claiming or destroying areas near other players' claims.

8. Respect all players and staff members.

9. Glitches should be reported, not abused.

10. No lag machines or anything that can ruin the general server experience for our players.

11. Staff have the final say.

12. Use common sense.

Apply for Moderator

Requirements:

1. At least 50 hours played on the server.

2. Active and mature, with a clear understanding of the server rules.

3. Must be willing to help players and enforce server rules in a fair and consistent manner.

4. Previous moderation experience is a plus.

Apply for Builder

Requirements:

1. Must have experience building in Minecraft (screenshots or portfolio required).

2. Ability to create aesthetically pleasing and functional builds.

3. Willingness to collaborate with other builders and staff.

4. Must be active and communicate well with the team.

FAQ

What is the server IP address?
The server's IP address is play.voidsent.net
What Minecraft version is the server running on?
The server is on 1.21.1+
How do I protect my builds from griefing?
You can claim land to protect your builds from other players
Is the survival world vanilla or does it have custom features?
The server has custom enchants and items along with many other plugins
Do you have an economy or trading system in the survival world?
Yes, we have different trading systems, some run by plugins, some run by players

You can earn money by selling items and you can collect resources by buying from the shop
How do I report issues or rule-breaking players?
